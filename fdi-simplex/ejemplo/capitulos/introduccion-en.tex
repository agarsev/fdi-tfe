\chapter{Introduction}

This chapter presents the background, objectives, and work plan of
the report, as required by the Faculty of Computer Science regulations
(\href{https://informatica.ucm.es/normativa-tfm-2425}{informatica.ucm.es/normativa-tfm-2425}).

\section{Background}

The Faculty of Computer Science at UCM has used the TeXiS~\cite{texis}
template for theses and final-year projects for over a decade. Over
time, the evolution of the \LaTeX{} ecosystem has left some of its
technical decisions outdated.

\section{Objectives}

The objectives of this work are:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Provide a modern template based on Lua\LaTeX.
  \item Separate normative aspects from aesthetic ones.
  \item Offer a minimal base that does not interfere with the
        packages each student chooses to use.
\end{itemize}

\section{Work Plan}

The plan covers the development of the class, the minimal and texis
styles, compilable examples, and verification against the current
regulations.
